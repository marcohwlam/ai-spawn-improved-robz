RobZ AI Overhaul - Smarter Bots, Real Waves (RobZ 1.30.x)

Your RobZ bots just got a brain transplant.

Tired of watching the AI dump its whole army in one suicidal blob, forget artillery exists, or leave three squads standing at base while the front line collapses. This mod rewrites the RobZ bot spawn and targeting logic from the ground up so it plays like an actual opponent, not a manpower faucet.

WHAT CHANGED

Dynamic wave spawning
No more manpower dumped the second it is earned. The bot banks up and deploys in coordinated waves, spread across ticks so the engine never rejects a burst, and the cadence reacts to the scoreboard. Falling behind on flags shortens the gap between waves and swells the budget (up to 2.5x), so the bot throws more at the fight the worse it is losing.

Smarter targeting
Groups push the frontier instead of leapfrogging past your lines into a deep, unsupported flag. A capture-settle grace period means the bot will not abandon a flag it just took the second the enemy shows up nearby.

Phase-aware composition
Early game is infantry and light armor. Mid game brings mediums. Late game the heavies roll out, all timed to real per-faction RobZ unlock schedules, not a generic guess.

Faction-flavored doctrine
Each faction now leans into its real WWII playbook: German armor spearheads, US artillery barrages, Soviet SMG assault waves, Japanese infiltration mortars. A guaranteed minimum for that faction's signature category is layered on top of the normal composition, not a random reshuffle.

Support units that actually show up
Assault guns escort the main push instead of sulking at a backline artillery flag. Gun-halftracks and scout cars get their own guaranteed keep-alive slot instead of losing every draw to seven duplicate MG entries.

Money-smart late game
If a heavy tank keeps failing to spawn because the bot cannot afford it, spawning pauses so manpower can actually bank up, instead of burning it all on filler while the heavy never lands.

Every trickle, tuned
Point-defense MGs, AT rifles, cappers, officers, deep-strike airborne, each runs its own capped, independent trickle instead of starving each other for a shared pool.

Crash-safe unit cap
The MoWAS2 engine is 32-bit. Flood the field with enough squads across a big team game and it will OOM crash. Each bot enforces a hard live-squad ceiling tuned to stay well under that limit, so a 2v2 or 3v3 match with AI on every side will not tip the game over.

COMPATIBILITY

- Lua-only, zero entity assets, will not conflict with content mods.
- Requires RobZ Realism Mod 1.30.10 (do not use older RobZ versions). Load this mod after RobZ so its bot overrides win.
- Game version 3.262.
- RobZ maps and units only. The engine bot API exposes flags and unit rosters, not a generic map format. Targeting runs on precalculated flag-sector data for the RobZ map pool (54 of 57 1v1/2v2/3v3 maps) plus this mod roster and unlock data for RobZ factions. A map outside that precalculated set falls back to degraded targeting. Other mods or vanilla maps are not supported.

WHY TRUST IT

Every fix ships with an offline test suite and gets cross-checked against RobZ own unit data before release. This is not guesswork, it is an AI that has actually been debugged.

CHANGE LOG

v1.1.0
- New: faction composition bias. Each faction guarantees a minimum field count for a signature category (German medium armor, US/British artillery, Soviet SMG assault infantry, Japanese mortars, and so on), layered on top of the existing composition ratio instead of replacing it.
- Fixed a spawn-confirmation desync that could misattribute one squad's confirmation to a different in-flight spawn and corrupt tier-ratio accounting.
- Fixed a freshly-created group getting deleted instead of retried when its first fill attempt failed, which could strand the bot on capper trickle alone for up to about 90s at match start.
- Fixed a pending-spawn-count leak on lost spawn confirmations that could wedge a group's fill slot.
- Corrected several ger_ss support-vehicle IDs that were silently failing to spawn (100% failure), added the missing SdKfz 251/10 support vehicle and LSSAH mechanized infantry entry.
- Mortars now get their own dedicated keep-alive trickle instead of competing untracked in the shared support pool.

v1.0.0
- Initial release: phase-based wave spawning, frontier-first group targeting, dedicated keep-alive trickles for support units/assault guns/artillery, late-game heavy-tank affordability guard, crash-safe unit cap.
